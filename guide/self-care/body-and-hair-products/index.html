Body & Hair Products

Stop using dollar-store soap. What to buy and why quality matters.

Most guys use whatever soap is cheapest at Target and wonder why their skin is dry or itchy. Body wash isn't a luxury—it's the difference between skin that feels and looks good and skin that feels like sandpaper. You're literally rubbing it on your skin every day. Invest $8 instead of $1.50.

The Three Body Wash Types

Charcoal/Cleansing — For oily skin or if you go to the gym. Removes buildup. Brands: Dove Men, Axe Essentials (surprisingly decent), Baxter Charcoal.
Hydrating — For dry skin or winter months. Leaves skin feeling soft. Brands: Cetaphil Pro, Dove Deep Moisture, Aveeno.
Exfoliating — Once or twice a week, not daily. Gets rid of dead skin and improves texture. Brands: Burt's Bees for Men, Clinique Face Soap Exfoliating.
Pick one type and stick with it for 4 weeks. Your skin adapts.

Shampoo & Conditioner Reality Check

Guys fall into two camps: (1) They use 2-in-1 and wonder why their hair looks flat and tangled. (2) They ignore conditioner entirely and have dry, brittle hair. The truth: your hair is dead protein. Shampoo opens the cuticle and strips oil. Conditioner closes it back down and protects. You need both.
Shampoo Types:
  • Clarifying — For oily hair or heavy product buildup. Once a week max.
  • Moisturizing — For dry hair. Doesn't strip as much. Most guys should use this.
Conditioner Rule: Use after every shampoo. Focus on the ends, not the roots. Leave it for 60 seconds. This single change makes hair look noticeably healthier in two weeks.

Why This Matters

Your hair is visible. Every day. Bad hair makes everything else look worse—even good clothes and a solid haircut. Shiny, healthy-looking hair is literally one of the first things people notice. Invest in decent shampoo and actually condition. You'll look better.

Key Takeaways

  • Dollar-store soap leads to dry or irritated skin; upgrade to mid-tier brands
  • Pick a body wash type and use it consistently for 4+ weeks
  • Conditioner after every shampoo is non-negotiable for good-looking hair
  • Clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup; moisturizing shampoo for daily use
  • Healthy hair is 80% of the "guy who has his life together" look
