Face Care Fundamentals

A routine you'll actually stick with. Clear skin is boring and boring is the goal.

You wash your hands multiple times a day. You shower. But most guys never properly clean their face. The result: acne, roughness, and a dull complexion that makes you look 5 years older. Clear skin isn't vanity—it's the baseline that every other part of your appearance sits on. Bad skin makes good clothes look worse.

The Minimum Viable Routine

Guys overcomplicate this. You don't need 10 steps. You need 4:
1. Cleanser — Once in the morning, once at night. Removes oil, dirt, and dead skin. Use lukewarm water (hot water strips your face). If you're oily: salicylic acid cleanser. If you're dry: a gentle, creamy cleanser.
2. Moisturizer — After cleansing. Hydration prevents oil production and keeps skin from looking dull. Morning moisturizer should have SPF.
3. Sunscreen — Morning only. Non-negotiable if you care about not looking aged in 10 years. Look for "facial sunscreen" not "body sunscreen"—lighter and won't clog pores.
4. Face Mask (Optional but High-ROI) — Once or twice a week. 10 minutes. Either clarifying (clay) or hydrating (sheet). Even one mask a week is the difference between okay skin and noticeably better skin.

Face Masks Explained

Most guys think face masks are ridiculous. Then they do one and their skin looks objectively clearer for three days. Two types:
Clay/Clarifying Masks — For oily or congested skin. Draws out impurities. You'll see visible improvement. Brands: Aztec Indian Healing Clay (ridiculously cheap), Biore Charcoal, or Origins Clear Improvement.
Sheet Masks — For dry or tired skin. Hydration delivered. Less dramatic but your face feels plump and healthy. Brands: Evercos, Mediheal, Dr. Jart.
Do it Sunday night or before something where you want to look sharp. Real results.

The Cologne Connection

Cologne sits on top of your skin. If your skin is rough, oily, or inflamed, cologne sits on top of problems. Clean, hydrated skin makes cologne smell better and last longer. It's why guys who use cologne + decent skincare always smell noticeably better.

Key Takeaways

  • Cleansing is non-negotiable; you're not washing properly in the shower alone
  • Moisturizer + SPF in the morning is the single best anti-aging move you can make
  • Clay masks once a week show visible results in just two weeks
  • Good skincare makes everything else (cologne, confidence, appearance) better
  • This routine takes 5 minutes; the ROI is massive
