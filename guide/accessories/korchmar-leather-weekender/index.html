THE BRAND

Who Makes This

Korchmar is a fourth-generation family-owned leather goods company (founded 1917) that still designs and manufactures its products in its own U.S.

and Dominican Republic facilities—no outsourcing, no fakes.

It made luggage for government and military early on, now quietly manufactures luxury goods for high-end clients while keeping its own line focused on function, materials, and craftsmanship.

They aren’t a flash-driven corporate giant—they’re underground legendary among people who care about quality, longevity, and American-made integrity.

HOW TO USE IT

Making It Work

Pair it with your minimal tailored travel wardrobe—clean chinos, an OCBD, and leather boots—and let the bag do the talking.

Use the clamp-down ends to cinch it tight for a tighter carry or release them when you need max capacity.

Every few months, treat the leather to a mild cream (Bick 4, Cadillac, or the brand’s own) to keep it supple and age-worthy.
