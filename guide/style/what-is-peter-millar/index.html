What Is Peter Millar?

The brand guys who get it already know about — and why you should.

What It Is

Peter Millar is an American luxury sportswear brand founded in 2001 in Raleigh, NC. It occupies the gap between Ralph Lauren and the $400-a-shirt European tier — presentable, performance-minded, and not interested in screaming a logo at you. Their two hero product lines are Crown Sport (moisture-wicking performance fabric built for golf and travel) and Soft Touch cotton (fine-gauge, wrinkle-resistant, passes for business casual without trying). They also manufacture the Augusta National merchandise exclusively, which is not available for general retail sale.

Why It Matters

Most guys hit a point where Polo feels too casual and too ubiquitous, but they're not ready to spend $250 on a shirt that requires explaining. Peter Millar fills that gap cleanly. The construction is genuinely a tier above department store sportswear — the fabric doesn't pill, the colors don't wash out, and Crown Sport wears through a full round of golf and straight into a dinner reservation without looking like either. It reads as intentional without being loud, which is harder to pull off than it sounds.

The GuyTalk Read

Buy it from the outlet (millarstore.com), not full price. The brand runs aggressive sales and the outlet carries current-season pieces regularly at 30–50% off — there's almost no reason to pay full retail. Fit runs slightly relaxed through the torso, so size down if you're between. The performance polo is the entry point everyone should own, the Crown Sport quarter-zip is the second purchase. If someone asks where your shirt is from and you say 'Peter Millar,' the follow-up is usually 'they make the Augusta stuff, right?' — and that's the correct answer.

What to Know
  • Crown Sport is real performance fabric, not marketing speak. Moisture-wicking, wrinkle-resistant, and legitimately looks better than most 'performance' polos that read as gym shirts.
  • Shop the outlet first (millarstore.com). Current-season pieces show up regularly at 30–50% off. Set a bookmark.
  • Size down. The cut runs slightly relaxed through the torso. If you're sitting between a medium and large, go medium.
  • Augusta National merchandise is made exclusively by Peter Millar and is only available at the club or gifted by members — not sold retail anywhere. If you see a member wearing it, now you know.
