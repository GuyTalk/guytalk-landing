THE GUYTALK TAKE

Our Honest Read

Creatine monohydrate has over 30 years of peer-reviewed research behind it and is the single most proven performance supplement in existence. Five grams a day, mixed into literally anything — water, coffee, your protein shake — increases your ability to produce force during short, intense efforts. That translates directly to heavier lifts, more reps, and faster recovery between sets.

The supplement industry runs on selling complexity: $80 pre-workouts with 20-ingredient stacks, proprietary blends with trademarked names, "advanced" creatine forms at 6× the price. All of it outperforms plain monohydrate in one category: marketing. In actual research, plain creatine monohydrate is as effective as every "enhanced" form, for a quarter a day.

Optimum Nutrition's unflavored monohydrate is the benchmark. No flavoring, no filler, 100 servings per 500g tub. It's the supplement that actually earns the counter space.

HOW TO USE IT

Making It Work

Mix one flat teaspoon (5g) into any liquid. It dissolves better in warm liquid but works fine cold. The timing doesn't matter — morning, pre-workout, post-workout, or before bed all produce the same result. What matters is taking it every day without skipping.

Expect a 2–3 lb weight gain in the first two weeks — that's water being pulled into muscle cells, which is the mechanism. Strength gains start becoming noticeable around weeks 3–4. If you stop taking it, those gains revert over 4–6 weeks.
