**Markets**

Microsoft's 5.4% rip on Friday was real—Satya Nadella's Copilot bet is finally printing money in enterprise deals, and Wall Street's repricing the AI narrative away from chip scarcity toward software that actually moves the needle. The gap between NVDA and MSFT tells you where the market thinks the real margin is, and it's getting harder to ignore that Jensen Huang's hardware play looks like a commodity business compared to Nadella's rent-seeking moat.

Watch next week for whether Microsoft's momentum holds or if this was a one-day relief pump.
