THE GUYTALK READ. NHL

Carolina Hurricanes 3–0 (CAR wins series 4-2)

What happened
Carolina Hurricanes shut out Vegas 3–0 to win the Stanley Cup Final 4–2.
Why it matters
Carolina finally broke through after decades of playoff heartbreak — the franchise has never won a Cup in its modern history. This is the first championship for a market that's been waiting since it relocated from Hartford in 1997.
The GuyTalk Read
Vegas got exposed when it mattered most. The Golden Knights built their rep on closing out series, but the Hurricanes' defense suffocated them when the stakes were highest. Carolina's goaltending and depth up front showed that big-money rosters don't always win in May. This is what a complete team looks like in June.
What to know
  • Vegas Golden Knights founded in 2017; never won a Cup before this run
  • Carolina Hurricanes relocated from Hartford in 1997; zero Stanley Cup titles in franchise history
  • Series went to Game 6; Hurricanes won final three games without allowing a goal
What to say
"Carolina just ended 29 years of organizational pain — nobody expected that shutout barrage."
