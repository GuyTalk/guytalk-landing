Microsoft tanks 4.2% as AI spending bets face reality check

Tech's having a weird day — the Magnificent Seven fracturing while the broadest index basically flatlines. MSFT got hammered 4.2% (earnings miss or guidance cut, the market's sending a message), but AAPL bounced 2.9% and TSLA ripped 1.9%, so it's not a sector rotation, it's a picking-winners game. Expect a relief rally that actually sticks.

Formula 1
Monaco Grand Prix · Circuit de Monaco · 78 laps through the streets of Monte Carlo · The most iconic 3.3 miles in motorsport

Monaco demands perfection. Verstappen's been perfect.

Street circuits don't forgive mistakes — one wall ends your day. Verstappen's been the guy who doesn't make them this season, but Monaco's always the race where even the best slip up. Watch who stays composed when the margin for error disappears.

  • Race:Monaco Grand Prix · Sunday
  • Watch:ESPN / ABC · Race day coverage begins ~9am ET
  • Driver to watch:Leclerc, because driving through your home race with the weight of a nation watching either breaks you or sharpens you, and he's the kind of driver who feeds off that pressure.
