Final Sharp Take

The tech selloff is real. NVDA and MSFT both down hard, the 10-year yield climbing—that's not rotation, that's caution. Meanwhile baseball is doing what it always does: blowouts everywhere, nobody's playing close games. The Mets, Tigers, and White Sox all won by five-plus runs today. When you see that kind of dispersion in sports and markets pulling back at the same time, the market's telling you it wants safety. Everything else is noise.
