Victor Wembanyama dropped 32 points and dished 6 assists while Jalen Brunson matched him point-for-point with 32 of his own, but San Antonio's depth was the difference. Stephon Castle added 23 points and Dylan Harper grabbed 9 rebounds off the bench as the Spurs took Game 3 115-111. New York still leads the series 2-1, but San Antonio just proved they can win without needing a blowout.

  • Why it matters: OG Anunoby gave the Knicks 28 points as an answer to Wembanyama, but New York had no answer for Castle and Harper combining for 36 points and 18 rebounds off the bench.
  • The other angle: Game 4 is next in New York, which means the Spurs have to survive a hostile Garden crowd or they head home down 3-1 with no margin for error the rest of the series.
  • What to say: Brunson and Wembanyama basically canceled each other out at 32 apiece, so this one came down to the benches — and Dylan Harper outrebounded half the Knicks starters.
