THE GUYTALK READ. US Open Golf 2026

Andrew Putnam clinches final U.S. Open qualifying spot after marathon playoff

What happened
Andrew Putnam won a marathon playoff to claim the 43rd and final qualifying spot for the 126th U.S. Open at Shinnecock Hills.
Why it matters
Putnam sneaked into the field while Scottie Scheffler enters the week needing a win to complete the career Grand Slam — meaning the guy who just barely made it could be watching the guy chasing history from the clubhouse.
What to say
"Putnam's going to Shinnecock as the 43rd seed and Scheffler's playing for immortality, which is exactly the kind of contrast that makes the U.S. Open what it is."
NBA Finals 2026 (Knicks Championship)
THE GUYTALK READ. NBA Finals 2026 (Knicks Championship)

Knicks win 2026 NBA championship, defeat Spurs in Game 5

What happened
Jalen Brunson scored 45 points and the Knicks won the 2026 NBA championship, defeating the Spurs in Game 5 to claim Finals MVP.
Why it matters
This ends a 53-year championship drought — the Knicks' first title since 1973 — and flips New York basketball from a punchline into something that actually matters again.
What to say
"Brunson showed up when it counted most and turned a city that's been waiting longer than most of us have been alive into champions."
FIFA World Cup 2026
THE GUYTALK READ. FIFA World Cup 2026

USMNT dominates Paraguay 4-1 in World Cup opener

What happened
Christian Pulisic led the USMNT to a 4-1 rout of Paraguay on June 12 at SoFi Stadium in Inglewood to open the 2026 World Cup.
Why it matters
The U.S. has only advanced past the Round of 16 once in the modern World Cup era — that was 24 years ago in 2002 — so a dominant opening like this signals the team might finally break through that glass ceiling.
What to say
"If the USMNT can actually string together tournament wins on home soil, this could be the run that rewrites their whole World Cup story."
