  • Kanye West and Travis Scott Banned From Performing in Italy
    Variety · Music/Culture

    Italy's interior ministry blocked concerts by both artists, citing security concerns and reputational risk tied to their public conduct. West faces ongoing legal and social fallout from antisemitic remarks; Scott's history includes crowd safety incidents. This isn't censorship theater—it's a country making a straightforward business decision that American promoters haven't had the spine to make. Tell someone: Italy just did what Ticketmaster won't.

  • Chad Gable Unmasked as El Grande Americano After AAA Loss
    Fightful · Wrestling

    Gable lost a mask vs. mask match at AAA Noches de los Grandes in Mexico, revealing he'd been working as a high-profile luchadore under an assumed identity. WWE allowed the crossover appearance, blurring lines between promotions in a way that used to be unthinkable. This matters because it signals wrestling's biggest company finally understands leverage comes from letting talent work, not controlling every appearance. You can drop this casually: "Did you see Gable just unmasked in Mexico? That's not the WWE you grew up with."
