Brazil vs. Scotland — Group C Showdown Today

What happened
Brazil takes the pitch against Scotland at 6 PM EDT today in what is shaping up to be the most-watched match of the group stage. Yesterday: Portugal demolished Uzbekistan 5–0 (Ronaldo ×2), England drew 0–0 with Ghana, Colombia edged Congo DR 1–0.
Why it matters
Brazil entering a World Cup with Vinícius Jr. and Endrick is a global event. Scotland, meanwhile, need a win to stay alive. England's toothless draw with Ghana is raising real questions about Tuchel's setup.
The GuyTalk Read
Yesterday proved the tournament gap is real. Portugal look like a machine when Ronaldo is healthy. England just do not look threatening enough going forward — and if they cannot break down Ghana, they are going to struggle against actual contenders. Today Brazil vs Scotland is the story.
What to know
  • Ronaldo scored in the 6th and 39th minutes against Uzbekistan
  • England's 0–0 draw with Ghana is their worst opening-stage performance since 2014
  • Brazil vs Scotland kicks off at 6 PM EDT — Vinícius Jr. starts

The Scene
  • Culture
    Clive Davis Dies at 94

    What happened: Clive Davis — the record executive who signed Whitney Houston, Barry Manilow, and Alicia Keys and essentially shaped four decades of American pop — died Tuesday at 94. The music industry lost arguably its most powerful tastemaker.

    Why it matters: If you've listened to pop, soul, or rock from the 1970s through the 2000s, Clive Davis had a hand in it. He's the reason Whitney Houston's debut sold 22 million copies.

    • Discovered Whitney Houston at 19 years old
    • Signed Bruce Springsteen, Janis Joplin, and Billy Joel at Columbia
    • Founded Arista Records in 1974; J Records in 2000
  • Culture
    Taylor Swift & Travis Kelce: Pre-Wedding Events

    What happened: Swift and Kelce were photographed together at an NFL off-season event Monday night. Reports from multiple outlets suggest a wedding is imminent, with both spending the weekend apart at what sources describe as pre-wedding activities.

    Why it matters: Whether you care about celebrity weddings or not, this is going to dominate pop culture conversation for the next several weeks. And Kelce's value as a brand ambassador — Nike, State Farm, etc. — just got exponentially larger.

    • Kelce is entering his 14th NFL season in 2026
    • Swift's Eras Tour grossed over billion globally
    • The couple has been together since summer 2023
  • Culture
    US-Iran Nuclear Deal

    What happened: The US and Iran announced a framework nuclear agreement Tuesday, with Iran agreeing to cap uranium enrichment at 60% in exchange for the lifting of key oil export sanctions. Oil spiked 4% on the news before reversing as traders weighed implementation risks.

    Why it matters: This is the biggest geopolitical deal since the 2015 JCPOA — and unlike the celebrity news cycle, this one directly affects gas prices, airline costs, and your energy portfolio. If the deal holds, oil supply increases and prices come down. If it falls apart, the Middle East tension trade is back on.

    • Brent crude spiked to $84/barrel before closing at $80 on the day
    • Iran has not accepted a uranium cap since the original 2015 deal
    • US sanctions relief would unlock approximately $7B in frozen Iranian assets
