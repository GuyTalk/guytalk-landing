    What happened: The new He-Man film opened and immediately became one of 2026's biggest box office bombs.

    What to say: He-Man is one of 2026's biggest box office bombs. At some point Hollywood has to admit that slapping a childhood property on a poster is not a business plan.

    NBA Finals Tickets Crater After Trump Confirms Attendance

    What happened: NBA Finals ticket prices dropped sharply after Donald Trump confirmed he would be attending a game.

    Why it matters: A sitting president's attendance usually drives demand up. The fact that it went the other way here tells you something specific about where the NBA fanbase's head is at right now.

    What to say: NBA Finals ticket prices crashed after Trump said he was going. A sitting president showing up usually makes tickets more expensive. Make of that what you will.
