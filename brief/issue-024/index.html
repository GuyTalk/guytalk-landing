Formula 1
Circuit de Monaco · Monte Carlo · The most iconic 3.3 miles in motorsport

Monaco's tight streets demand perfection. Max owns it.

◍Circuit de Monaco · Monte Carlo
  • Why it matters: Monaco separates the elite drivers from everyone else because there's zero margin for error on those narrow streets.
  • Championship: Verstappen's won here before and he thrives when the pressure is highest and the track punishes mistakes.
  • Watch for: First-lap chaos in Turn 1. Monaco's opening corner determines half the race before lap two.
  • What to say: Monaco's the one race where qualifying basically decides everything. You can't pass there, so nail Saturday and you're golden Sunday.
Golf
Muirfield Village Golf Club · Dublin, Ohio · Host of The Memorial Tournament

Memorial Tournament kicks off at Muirfield Village this week

◍Muirfield Village Golf Club · Thu, June 3rd at 12:00 AM EDT
  • Why it matters: Muirfield Village is Jack Nicklaus's course and one of the toughest tests on tour—winners here have real credentials.
  • The angle: This is the last major prep event before the US Open in two weeks, so form matters.
  • In the running: Who stays patient on the greens. Muirfield Village punishes aggression and rewards guys who lag-putt properly.
  • What to say: Memorial separates the pretenders from guys who can actually compete on brutally hard courses.
