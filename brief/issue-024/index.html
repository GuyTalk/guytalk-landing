Golf

Memorial Tournament kicks off at Muirfield Village this week

  • Why you should care: Muirfield Village is Jack Nicklaus's course and one of the toughest tests on tour—winners here have real credentials.
  • Course angle: This is the last major prep event before the US Open in two weeks, so form matters.
  • Watch for: Who stays patient on the greens. Muirfield Village punishes aggression and rewards guys who lag-putt properly.
  • What to say: Memorial separates the pretenders from guys who can actually compete on brutally hard courses.
Formula 1
Monaco Grand Prix · Circuit de Monaco · 78 laps through the streets of Monte Carlo · The most iconic 3.3 miles in motorsport

Monaco's tight streets demand perfection. Max owns it.

  • Why it matters: Monaco separates the elite drivers from everyone else because there's zero margin for error on those narrow streets.
  • Championship: Verstappen's won here before and he thrives when the pressure is highest and the track punishes mistakes.
  • Watch for: First-lap chaos in Turn 1. Monaco's opening corner determines half the race before lap two.
  • What to say: Monaco's the one race where qualifying basically decides everything. You can't pass there, so nail Saturday and you're golden Sunday.

The Rec
This week's pick
Oura Ring Gen 3 — sleep tracking for guys who won't wear a wristband.

Oura tracks sleep stages, body temperature, and readiness in a ring that looks like jewelry. Battery lasts 5–7 days. The daily readiness score is accurate enough to schedule around — if it says 45%, you probably feel like 45%. The honest trade-off: the ring is $349 upfront plus $6/month for the app, and the heart-rate workout tracking is genuinely inferior to Apple Watch or Garmin. If you want sleep data and don't want a wristband, it's the best option. If you want workout tracking too, get the Whoop instead.
