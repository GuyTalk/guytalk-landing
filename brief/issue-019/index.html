Charles Schwab Challenge: Russell Henley wins at -13.

Russell Henley won the Charles Schwab Challenge at 13-under par.

  • Leaderboard (Final):Russell Henley -13 (1st), Eric Cole -12 (2nd), Ben Griffin -11 (3rd), Alex Smalley -11 (4th), Mac Meissner -11 (5th).
  • Why it matters:Henley's wire-to-wire at Colonial positions him as a legitimate major threat heading into the U.S. Open in two weeks, and this is his second win in three weeks—he's playing the best golf of his career right now.
  • How it happened:Henley shot -13 to win by one over Eric Cole, who finished at -12. Ben Griffin, Alex Smalley, and Mac Meissner all tied for third at -11, meaning three guys within two shots couldn't catch him down the stretch on a course that usually rewards aggression.
