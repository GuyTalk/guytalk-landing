# GuyTalk Issue-Style Social System

The best GuyTalk posts should look like cropped pieces of the actual brief, not generic social graphics.

## Core Frame

Use this structure for most static posts and carousels:

1. Small top bar: `GuyTalk.` left, date or issue number right
2. Category tag: `SPORTS`, `MARKETS`, `CULTURE`, or `SHARP TAKE`
3. Big headline: one clear thought
4. TL;DR box: 2-3 short bullets
5. Bottom line: `What to bring up:` or `Why it matters:`
6. Footer: `guytalkmedia.com`

## Visual Rules

- Background: `#0B1322`
- Card/surface: `#131D30`
- Border: `#1F2B43`
- Primary text: `#F2F4F7`
- Secondary text: `#8B96AB`
- Accent blue: `#3D7EFF`
- Use mono labels for tags, dates, tickers, and scores.
- Avoid big centered slogan cards except for the first intro post.
- Make posts feel like a product screenshot of the brief.
