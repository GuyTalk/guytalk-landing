## Brand Lock

- The brand is `GuyTalk`, never `GuyTalk Media`.
- The logo is `GuyTalk.` with the blue period.
- The tiny/square mark is `g.`.
- Keep the identity flat, dark, sharp, and product-like.

## Site Design Notes

- The first screen should sell the actual brief, not a generic newsletter.
- CTA language should feel like joining early access, not just subscribing.
- Keep the sample brief visible and close to the top.
- Do not overbuild an app promise yet. Hint at alerts and app direction, but the newsletter is the MVP.
