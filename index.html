How it works

Every story. Three layers.

GuyTalk takes what happened and gives you the context, the stakes, and the take — every time.

01
What happened.
Every story starts with a clean account of what actually went down. Sharp, direct, no spin. You read it, you know it.
"Nvidia crossed $4 trillion in market cap this week."
02
Why it matters.
Not just the news — the context that makes it relevant to your actual conversations. Why this story, why now, why you should care.
"It's not just a stock move. Every major tech company guided AI capex higher — Nvidia is the only one selling the shovels."
03
What to bring up.
The office take. The bar argument. The group chat angle. We hand you the angle that lands in any conversation — so you walk in ready.
"The real story isn't Nvidia's price. It's that Microsoft, Google, and Meta are all still accelerating infrastructure spend. That's what's driving it."

Why I built this

I was spending two hours a day scrolling — and somehow still felt like I was behind. ESPN had endless hot takes. Morning Brew talked finance like I was a banker. Twitter was a war zone. Nothing was built for me.

Built for guys who want to walk into the day informed, not exhausted. If that sounds like you, join below.
