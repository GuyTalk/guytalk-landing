## 1. Positioning & messaging

- **Name:** GuyTalk™ · **Domain:** guytalkmedia.com
- **Category:** The daily relevance brief — sports, markets, culture.
- **Promise:** *Don't be the last guy to know.* Know what happened, why it matters,
  and what to bring up — in 5 minutes.
- **ICP:** Men ~25–45 who want to feel informed and hold their own in any room
  (group chat, meeting, bar, date) without losing their morning to the feed.
- **Why it's different:** Not just *what* happened — the **angle** and the **line to
  say**. Plus a live `/live` dashboard no other newsletter has.

**Message hierarchy (use everywhere):**
1. Hook: "Don't be the last guy to know."
2. Proof: sports · markets · culture, 5 min, free, no algorithm.
3. Wedge: the "what to say" / bar‑argument line you can repeat today.

## 2. The growth thesis (the wedge)

The single most shareable thing GuyTalk produces is **the line you repeat to a friend.**
"Did you see the Spurs stole Game 3?" → "where'd you hear that?" → "this 5‑min email."

So the whole engine optimizes for **one screenshot‑able, repeatable take per day** that
travels by word of mouth and social share. Everything points back to a free signup.

Three compounding loops:
1. **Social → signup** (organic posts repurposing the brief).
2. **Referral** (readers invite friends; Beehiiv built‑in).
3. **Word of mouth** (the "what to say" line spreads offline → searches → signup).

> welcome email + referral loop are live. Pushing traffic onto a leaky funnel wastes it.

## 4. Channels, ranked by leverage (solo founder, ~$0)

### Tier 1 — do these first (highest leverage, lowest cost)
1. **Short‑form video (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts).**
   The brief's daily "hot take" / "what to say" line is native to short video.
   Use the Higgsfield pipeline + talking‑head/text hooks. 1–2/day. This is the
   single biggest org‑growth lever for this audience in 2026.
2. **Manual, unscalable outreach for the first 100.**
   DM/text the brief to relevant people; post the daily take in group chats, relevant
   Discords/Slacks, and subreddits where it's genuinely useful (r/sports, r/stocks,
   r/Fantasy_Football in‑season — *contribute the take, don't spam a link*).
3. **Referral loop.** Turn on Beehiiv referral; reward at 1 / 3 / 5 invites. Put the
   ask at the bottom of every issue ("know a guy who's always last to know?").

### Tier 2 — build in parallel
4. **X/Twitter.** Reply‑guy strategy on big sports/markets moments with the GuyTalk
   take + link in bio. Post the daily card. Quote‑tweet breaking news with the angle.
5. **Newsletter cross‑promo / swaps.** Trade shout‑outs with adjacent men's‑interest
   / sports / finance newsletters of similar size. Beehiiv Boosts marketplace too.
6. **SEO via the archive + `/live`.** Every issue is an indexable page; `/live` is a
   genuinely linkable utility ("live scores + markets in one place"). Slow but compounding.

### Tier 3 — later, once conversion is proven
7. **Paid tests.** Small TikTok Spark Ads / Meta budgets ($5–20/day) *only after*
   organic creative shows what hooks convert. Scale what works.
8. **Creator partnerships.** Pay/seed micro‑creators in sports/finance to run the
   "what to say" format.

## 7b. Tracking — UTMs & the funnel (set up 2026‑06‑10)

**Funnel insight (live in PostHog):** "Visitor → Signup" — `$pageview → email_signup`.
Currently **78 visitors → 3 signups = 3.85%** (90 days). Watch this number; gate the
paid push on it holding ≥3–5% once real traffic arrives.
→ https://us.posthog.com/project/428450/insights/hHGnJ72r

**UTM convention** — tag every external link so PostHog attributes the source. Format:
`https://www.guytalkmedia.com/?utm_source=<where>&utm_medium=<type>&utm_campaign=<what>`

| Channel | utm_source | utm_medium |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | `tiktok` | `social` |
| Instagram | `instagram` | `social` |
| X | `x` | `social` |
| Newsletter swap | `<partner>` | `swap` |
| Reddit/forum | `reddit` | `community` |
| Paid | `tiktok`/`meta` | `paid` |

Tracked events already firing: `$pageview`, `email_signup`, `brief_read`, `404_hit`.

## 8. 30 / 60 / 90‑day plan

**Days 1–30 — Foundations + first 100 (manual)**
- Turn on welcome email + referral; add share/referral asks to the brief.
- Ship the PostHog funnel + UTM links.
- Post 1 short video/day (the daily take). Find the 2–3 hook formats that land.
- Personally onboard the first 100 (network, group chats, relevant communities).
- Start replying on X to big sports/markets moments.

**Days 31–60 — Find the repeatable channel**
- Double down on whichever short‑video hook converts; cut the rest.
- Line up 2–3 newsletter swaps / Beehiiv Boosts.
- First "best lines of the week" recap. Begin collecting **real** testimonials.
- Tighten visitor→signup toward 5%+.

**Days 61–90 — Amplify what works**
- If a video format reliably converts, start small paid tests behind it.
- Formalize the referral milestones; feature top referrers.
- Evaluate: which channel has the lowest cost per confirmed subscriber? Pour in there.
